Franz Lab Impact on Admission

Research Group membership starting Spring 2026 materially changes your competitive position — but the effect is not uniform.

Very Strong Impact

MS Clinical Research

Hands-on NMR/HPLC/mass spec, drug delivery project, and a named PI letter directly address what these programmes value. Transforms borderline into competitive.

Very Strong Impact

MS Regulatory Affairs

Analytical chemistry skills are core regulatory science competencies. Drug delivery project directly applicable. Distinguishes application in a technical applicant pool.

Strong Impact

PharmD

Molecular drug narrative, medicinal chemistry background, and chemistry faculty letter differentiates from the typical applicant pool. Moves strong to stronger.

Strong Impact

MEPN

Research experience is rare in nursing applicant pools. Prof. Franz letter is an institutional advantage specifically at UC Davis. Moves average to above-average.

Moderate Impact

MS Biomedical Informatics

Lab data management and quantitative methods are relevant but less decisive than for Clinical Research MS. Helpful, not transformative.

Moderate Impact

MPH

Adds credibility for quantitative/epidemiology tracks. Already accessible — Franz lab provides minor additional benefit but is not a game-changer for MPH admissions.

Moderate Impact

MS Nursing Science (post-MEPN)

Research experience before and during MEPN distinguishes within the nursing research track. Not applicable until after RN licensure.

No Meaningful Impact

MS Healthcare Admin

Administration programmes are driven by organisational, leadership, and management experience — not laboratory research skills.

Key Takeaway Leverage Franz lab most aggressively in applications that explicitly value research methods and scientific rigour — MS Clinical Research and MS Regulatory Affairs. Use it as a differentiator in PharmD and MEPN pools. Do not rely on it for healthcare admin or standalone MPH applications.

Immediate Action Plan

Sequenced steps from February 2026 through application season. GPA comes first.

⚠ Workload Warning You cannot simultaneously commit to 150 pharmacy tech hours, 200+ nursing clinical hours, Franz lab, and GPA protection without risk. Overcommitting in Year 3 may damage the GPA that makes all these paths viable. Prioritise GPA first; accumulate hours in gaps and summers.
This Week — February 2026

Confirm Franz Lab Start

Lock in start date, project, and weekly hours with Prof. Franz. Target 6–8 hrs/wk (drop to 4–6 hrs/wk during GPA-critical Fall 2026 quarter).

April 2026

Begin Pharmacy Tech Licence & Applications

Apply for positions by April 2026. Prioritise clinical settings: Kaiser Permanente Fremont, UC Davis Health, hospital inpatient pharmacies. Retail acceptable only if clinical roles unavailable.

Now → Summer 2026 (if MEPN is under consideration)

Register as Clinical Volunteer

UC Davis Medical Center or Fremont-area hospital. Even 2–4 hrs/wk now builds the nursing clinical portfolio (target 150–300 direct patient care hours by Summer 2027) in parallel.

Summer 2026

Audit MEPN Prerequisite Status

Confirm whether Statistics (STA 13/100), Nutrition (NUT 10/11), Lifespan Development (HDE 100A/B/C), and Oral Communication (CMN 1/5/120) are completed. Schedule any missing courses in Summer 2026 or Winter 2027.

Summer 2026 (if Informatics is of interest)

Add Introductory Programming

Python or R. Not required for all informatics programmes, but strengthens the application meaningfully. Consider online coursework during lighter quarters.

Winter–Spring 2027

Review Target Programme Pages

MS Regulatory Affairs: USC, SFSU, UCSF. MS Clinical Research: UCSF CTSI, UC Davis CTS. MS Informatics: UCSF, UCSD. Identify letters of recommendation needed for each path.

Spring 2027 — Critical Decision Point

Choose Primary Path & Begin Personal Statement Drafting

PharmCAS (pharmacy) and NursingCAS (nursing) require distinct narratives and different letter-writer profiles. You must commit to a primary identity. The decision should be grounded in your clinical hour experiences in both pharmacy and nursing settings accumulated to this point.

July 2027

Submit PharmCAS (if PharmD primary)

Submit pharmacy school applications. Target UCSF, UCSD, UCI, USC. Strong application: Franz lab letter, pharmacy tech hours, NPB prereq completion, 3.55–3.75 GPA.

November 2027

Submit NursingCAS (if MEPN parallel or primary)

Target UC Davis, UCI, USC. Requires distinct personal statement emphasising nursing identity — not a rephrased pharmacy essay.
